# ADR 009: Artist-led public design and restrained administration

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-07-15

## Decision

Public sites use semantic design tokens and artist-controlled composition. The demonstration begins with an editorial, image-led, cardless music experience. Administration uses neutral utility language, open layout, and minimal chrome.

## Why

Artists need a distinctive home rather than a visibly generic SaaS template. Operational surfaces need clarity and status more than marketing language.

## Consequences

Every public surface begins with a visual thesis, content plan, and interaction thesis. Cards communicate selectable items or functional boundaries only. Artist customization remains constrained by accessibility requirements. See `docs/architecture/visual-direction.md`.
